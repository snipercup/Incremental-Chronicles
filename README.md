# Incremental Chronicles
 Incremental Chronicles is a strategic, text-based incremental idle game where every reincarnation shapes your destiny. Progress through tiered areas, upgrade your stats, and uncover hidden lore generated by a dynamic AI. Choose between pushing forward or accepting early reincarnation for powerful bonuses that carry over to future runs. Convert repeated actions into permanent traits like Wisdom and Perception, making each reincarnation more powerful. Align with factions, interact with evolving NPCs, and face increasingly complex challenges. Will you ascend, conquer, or unite the kingdom?
